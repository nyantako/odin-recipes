Pancakes

Description

Today I will be showing you how to make some cheeky pancakes for all your hungry needs!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • White Sugar
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Sift dry ingredients together
  2. Then add the wet ingredients, stir to combine
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot pan
  4. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes and then flip
  5. Continue cooking until brown on each side
  6. Enjoy with some syrup!
