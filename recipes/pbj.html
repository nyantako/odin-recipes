PB&J

Description

Today I will be showing you how to make a PB&J or a peanut butter and jelly!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Strawberry or Grape)
  • Bread of your liking

Steps

  1. Grab a knife and your Bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread jelly on the other side
  4. COMBINE!!!!
